# Racing Car
Get ready for the ultimate adrenaline rush in Car Racing, the most thrilling and action-packed racing car game on the market! Step into the driver's seat and rev up your engine as you embark on an electrifying journey through a world of high-speed competition and heart-pounding excitement.

This is a simple racing car game made with Unity3D and C#. The goal of the player is to drive around in an racing track. There will be 2 races in 2 different terrains waiting for you to explore.

## Gameplay
The player controls a car that must drive around the track to reach the finish line without being overtaken by another car or going off the track.

- Move: W, S, A, D
- Brake (Dift): space
